Getting Started: How to Use Discover Events

Searching for Events:

• Enter keywords in the search bar on the homepage.
• Use filters for date, event type, or location to refine your search.

Registering for an Account:

• Click the 'Sign Up' button on the top right corner.
• Fill in the required fields and create a password.
• Confirm your email to activate your account.

Finding Event Details:

• Click on any event listing to view its details.
• Check the date, time, location, and description.

Saving and Sharing Events:

• Use the 'Save' feature to keep track of your favorite events.
• Share events with friends through social media links.

Find answers to commonly asked questions about using our service, event listings, account management, and more.
